About Me

My name is Nebiyou Kebede and was born and raised In Addis Ababa (Ethiopia). I moved to the United States in 2012

I Moved to the United States in search of a better opportunity, better life and to pursue my education. Moving to the United States was not a walk in the park. Once I moved I had to endure the wheatear and the culture shock, The language barrier and finding a stable job.It was a big challenge for a new Comer but through time i came to adjust.

Once I got a stable job and saved enough money, I was ready to fulfill my longtime dream which is to finish my education that I started back home, and I got the opportunity to attend University of Pennsylvania to improve and continue my education in Computer Technology.
